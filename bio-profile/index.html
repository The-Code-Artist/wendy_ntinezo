Wendy's BioProfile

Want to know more about Wendy? Then check her out below.

  • Wendy Begins

    Wendy Thembisile Ntinezo is an Entrepreneur, Host & Actress who runs a company "Styled Timeless Productions (Pty) Ltd". A CocaCola advert is where she kick-started her career at the of age 6 and took a break thereafter. As the years went on she kept sowing more seeds from where she left off and most (if not all) of the seeds she's been sowing throughout the years are now ripe and ready for Wendy's indulgence at her discretion of course.

    ~1999

  • Back to Theatre

    At 16, Wendy resumed her career after the break she took by enrolling in a theatre academy (South African Musical Theatre Academy) in Johannesburg where she began the journey of sharpening her skills for acting as well as setting the stage for what was to follow.

    ~2009

  • Thriving in Theatre

    Wendy took part in various plays like Honk Jr, Pirates of Penzance and Anne to name a few. She participated in a youth business initiative (YiBees) with a twofold purpose, amassing insight and experience in the business aspect of theatre as well as succeeding in the fund-raising for one of the aforementioned plays (Pirates of Penzance).

    ~2010

  • Act put together

    Wendy was in an agency (Jaffit Management) where she obtained an acting coach (Jani van der Westhuizen) who effectively upped the ante on her acting talent. She later had the privilege of obtaining "one-on-one" sessions with Pamela Nomvete where she persistently kept sharpening her skills for acting and maintained a seamless attendance record for the sessions to top things off.

    ~2013

  • The Growth

    2013 & 2014 are the years where Wendy put major forks on her road, as simple as it sounds, the progress made between these two years contributed to forging the diverse, adaptive persona which she possess at present. Spanning from late 2013 to 2014 she served as a promoter for the following: Mbongi Works, Magnum Pop up Store, Oreo Pop up store as well as the Cadbury pop up store. In 2014 she rolled the dice into double sixes by working with one of South Africa's greatest Directors (Paul Noko) on a play (The Bucket). She went on to working on a campaign pertaining to road safety where she got the opportunity to serve as Drama Facilitator for 6 months at Bopasnatla High School in Soweto. The school was awarded Best Performance Of The Year as a result.

    ~2013 - 2014

  • The Entrepreneurial Journey

    This where Wendy ventures out into business and as a result she registered her company Styled Timeless Productions (Pty) Ltd as well as her brand Wendy which you're currently viewing on your browsing device. Any campaign requested from the brand Wendy is done under the aforementioned company Styled Timeless Productions (Pty) Ltd which also functions as an umbrella for the brand Wendy.

    ~2015

  • The Portfolio

    Around 2015 through 2017 is the period where Wendy began refining the herbs in preparation for the savoury sections of her professional portfolio. Wendy began working with the likes of Levi's, CottonOn, Woolworths, Novesta, Adidas, Estee Lauder, and Dark & Lovely serving as an influencer and model for the brands respectively. An opportunity knocked on Wendy's door and she opened it and served as a presenter for the fashion segment in a television show (The A-List) and we all know the implications of such an undertaking. So watch this space...

    ~2015 - 2017

  • Wendy Arrives

    How much more solid does a foundation for a sound career have to be? It is in 2018 that Wendy had the opportunity of being part of the South African acquisition of Novesta in Slovakia. Wendy was mainly involved throughout the acquisition process and the marketing strategy as well. She then served as a model for the Novesta South Africa spring collection whilst in Prague, Czech Republic. Wendy is currently the Executive Producer, Producer as well as the Host for an online TV show which focusses on talks about spirituality. The show is titled Conversations We Never Had. So be sure not to confuse it with the book also bearing the same title. Be sure to keep watch on this Phenomenal being, for she has so much more to deliver...

    2018 onwards...
