Who's Wendy?

Wendy Thembisile Ntinezo is the epitome of a diverse, flexible and adaptive persona. Her polymorphous persona gives her the ability to rock any personality like a lady who's ever appealing regardless of the style or flavour of the clothing she decides to wear. Humour flows from her day-to-day personality so as to shove aside the "uptight/ serious" aspect of life without ditching the discipline to "get things done". This Actress, Entrepreneur and TV Host is what one would refer to as the "premium full package" in the business.
